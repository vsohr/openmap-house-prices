The Problem

Property investors need to identify growth areas and compare price trends across the UK, but existing tools are either behind paywalls, show only asking prices (not actual sold prices), or lack geographic granularity.

Current Pain Points

  • Rightmove/Zoopla show asking prices, not real sale prices
  • No free interactive map of actual transaction data
  • Hard to spot emerging growth areas at a glance
  • Comparing districts requires manual lookup across multiple sites

What Investors Want

  • Real sold prices from official government records
  • Visual map to spot growth trends quickly
  • Year-over-year comparisons across districts
  • Breakdown by property type (houses vs flats)

A free, open-source interactive map powered entirely by official government data.

Key Features

Choropleth Map

Colour-coded districts showing year-over-year price growth. Blue for falling, red for rising. Spot trends at a glance.

District Details

Click any district to see average price, YoY change, transaction volume, and regional average days to sell.

Price Trend Charts

Historical price chart per district with adjustable year range. See how prices evolved over decades.

Property Type Breakdown

Filter by Detached, Semi-detached, Terraced, or Flats. Compare average prices by building type.

Search & Navigate

Search by postcode or area name. The map flies to the selected district with a smooth animation.

Compare Districts

Side-by-side comparison of two districts. Evaluate investment targets against each other.
